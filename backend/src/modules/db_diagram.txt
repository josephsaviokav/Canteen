user /
userId (PK) | firstName | lastName | phone (UNIQUE) | email (UNIQUE) | password | role 

category /
categoryId (PK) | categoryName (UNIQUE) | categoryDescription 

item /
itemId (PK) | categoryId (FK) | itemName | description | price | imageUrl | stockQuantity | isAvailable

cart /
cartId (PK) | userId (FK)

cart_item /
cartItemId (PK) | cartId (FK) | itemId (FK) | quantity 

order
orderId (PK) | userId (FK) | totalAmount | placedAt | status (pending/confirmed/cancelled)

order_item
orderItemId (PK) | orderId (FK) | itemId (FK) | quantity | unitPrice | subtotal

payment
paymentId (PK) | orderId (FK, UNIQUE) | userId (FK) | paymentMethod (upi/card/cash/wallet) | amount | status (pending/success/failed)

relationship
users 1 --- 1 carts
users 1 --- many orders
users 1 --- many payments
categories 1 --- many items
carts 1 --- many cart_items
orders 1 --- many order_items
orders 1 --- 1 payments
items 1 --- many cart_items
